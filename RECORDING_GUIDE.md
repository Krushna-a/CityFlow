## Audio Recording Tips

### Voiceover Recording (After Screen Recording)

**Setup:**
1. Open Audacity or your DAW
2. Select USB mic or internal mic
3. Do 30-second test recording
4. Play back and check levels (should peak around -6dB)

**Recording Process:**
1. Have script in front of you
2. Read entire script once (don't stop for mistakes)
3. If mistakes, pause 2 seconds and re-record that section
4. Maintain consistent volume
5. Don't eat/drink while recording
6. Speak clearly and at 150-160 wpm

**Post-Recording:**
1. Export as WAV (uncompressed)
2. Normalize audio (-3dB target)
3. Add fade in/fade out (500ms each)
4. Import into video editor
